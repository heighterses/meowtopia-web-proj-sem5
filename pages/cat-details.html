Luna

Age: 2 years
Breed: Domestic Shorthair
Gender: Female
Color: Calico

Luna's Story

Meet Luna, a beautiful calico cat with a heart full of love and a story that will touch your soul. Luna was found wandering the streets during a cold winter night, seeking shelter and warmth. Our rescue team discovered her huddled under a cardboard box, and it was love at first sight.

Despite her rough start, Luna has blossomed into a gentle and affectionate companion. She loves to curl up in laps, play with feather toys, and watch birds from the window. Her favorite pastime is chasing laser dots and taking long naps in sunny spots.

Personality Traits

  • Affectionate and loving
  • Playful and energetic
  • Gets along well with other cats
  • Loves attention and cuddles

Health & Care

  • Spayed and up to date on vaccinations
  • Microchipped for safety
  • Regular vet check-ups
  • Healthy appetite and active lifestyle

Adoption Information

Luna is ready to find her forever home! She would do best in a loving family environment where she can receive plenty of attention and playtime. She's adaptable to both apartment and house living, as long as she has space to play and explore.

Requirements

  • Indoor-only living environment
  • Regular playtime and exercise
  • Quality cat food and fresh water
  • Loving and patient family
